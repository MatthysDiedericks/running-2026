⚠️ Treadmill vs Garmin — the truth:
Your Garmin reads 25–30% short on the treadmill because the wrist accelerometer estimates stride length from arm swing — it degrades badly indoors. Always use the treadmill belt display for your time and distance, not Garmin. When entering treadmill times above, use what the treadmill machine shows. Set 1% incline to match road air-resistance — that gives you a road-equivalent training stimulus and zeroes the correction factor.
